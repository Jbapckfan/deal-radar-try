/ questions

Before you ask.

How is this different from Honey or Slickdeals?

Those track a product URL or surface whatever's trending — they have no idea what size you wear. Dibs starts with your size and only ever shows things that are in stock in it, above the discount you set, from the brands you picked.

What if a brand I want isn't tracked yet?

Tap “Request a brand.” Most stores add themselves the same day automatically. If one can't be tracked, you'll know right away — and we'll point you to a similar brand we do follow. No dead ends.

How do you know my size across so many brands?

We map each brand's own sizing to yours, and learn your fit over time — so if you're a Medium most places but a Large in the ones that run small, the feed still gets it right. Every deal shows the brand's exact size so you can double-check.

Is it really free?

Yes. The core radar is free forever. Pro adds instant alerts, multiple size profiles and price history. When you buy from a deal we may earn a small commission from the retailer — it never changes your price.

iPhone only?

iPhone first. A web version and Android are on the roadmap once the core is dialed in.

How fast are the alerts?

Free gets a daily digest of everything new in your sizes. Pro pings you the moment a deal crosses your line — which matters, because the good stuff in popular sizes sells out fast.
